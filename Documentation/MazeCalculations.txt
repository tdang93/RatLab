Cell = 18cm x 18cm
Maze = 16 x 16 Tiles

1 unit = 1 Cell length = 18cm
1 Cell = 1 unit x 1 unit
Maze = 16 units x 16 units

( From: http://site.ieee.org/r1/files/2013/03/2013-Region-1-Micromouse-Competition-Rules.pdf )
The maze is composed of multiples of an 18 cm x 18 cm unit square. The maze comprises 16 x 16 unit squares. The walls of the maze are 5 cm high and 1.2 cm thick (assume 5% tolerance for mazes). The outside wall encloses the entire maze.
